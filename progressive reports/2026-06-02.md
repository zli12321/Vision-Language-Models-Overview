## 1. New SoTA & Unified Models

> README latest entry: **LensVLM (Apple/Duke), Nemotron 3 Nano Omni (NVIDIA), LLaDA2.0-Uni, PLaMo 2.1-VL — 05/07/2026** (previous progressive report).

| Model | Date | Architecture | Training Data | Parameters | Vision Encoder | Pretrained Backbone |
|-------|------|-------------|---------------|------------|----------------|---------------------|
| [Mamoda2.5 (InclusionAI)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02641) | 05/04/2026 | AR-Diffusion + DiT-MoE (unified I/O, Era 3b) | Multimodal understanding + image/video generation & editing | 25B total · 3B active (128 experts, Top-8) | Semantic tokenizer + DiT decoder head | Mamoda2 |
| [VLM3 (native 3D)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30561) | 05/28/2026 | Standard decoder-only VLM (no architecture change) | Mixed 2D/3D task data with focal-length unification | Backbone-agnostic recipe | Standard ViT | Any standard VLM |

### Highlights

- **Mamoda2.5** is a unified understanding-and-generation model built on an **AR-Diffusion** framework with a Diffusion-Transformer Mixture-of-Experts backbone (128 experts, Top-8 routing; 25B total / 3B active). It reaches top-tier generation on **VBench 2.0**, sets a new record in video-editing quality (matching the proprietary Kling O1), and — via distillation plus RL that compresses the editing model from 30 steps to 4 — delivers **up to 95.9× faster video-editing inference** than open-source alternatives. A strong data point for the Era-3b omni-modal-unified-I/O branch.
- **VLM3** argues that "Vision Language Models Are Native 3D Learners": rather than new architectures, large models, or heavy augmentation, just three ingredients — **focal-length unification, text-based pixel reference, and data-mixture scaling** — let an ordinary VLM master depth estimation, pixel correspondence, camera-pose estimation, and object-level 3D understanding. Depth accuracy rises from **0.84 → 0.90**, matching expert vision models. This reframes 3D as a data/representation problem rather than an architectural one.

### 3.1 RL Alignment for Multimodal Generation & VLM

> README latest entry: **S-GRPO, Faithful GRPO, GRPO-TTA, OpenSearch-VL — 05/2026** (previous progressive report).

| Title | Date | Paper | RL Method | Code |
|-------|------|-------|-----------|------|
| AlphaGRPO: Self-Reflective Multimodal Generation in UMMs via Decompositional Verifiable Reward | 05/12/2026 | [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12495) | GRPO for **unified multimodal models**; an LLM decomposes each request into atomic, verifiable semantic/quality questions to provide stable generation reward | — |

#### Key Insights
- **AlphaGRPO** extends GRPO from understanding/reasoning into **image generation** for unified multimodal models — the hard part being a stable reward for open-ended generation. Its **Decompositional Verifiable Reward** breaks a prompt into atomic checkable questions, yielding consistent gains on GenEval, TIIF-Bench, DPG-Bench, and WISE, plus an *emergent* improvement on editing despite no editing training data. Together with Mamoda2.5's RL-based step distillation, RL is now a first-class tool for the *generation* side of VLMs, not just reasoning.

## Key Trends Observed

1. **RL moves into the *generation* half of unified models.** AlphaGRPO brings GRPO to image generation in UMMs via a Decompositional Verifiable Reward, while Mamoda2.5 uses RL-driven step distillation (30→4 steps, 95.9× faster editing). Reinforcement learning is no longer just a reasoning/alignment tool — it is now shaping how unified models *produce* pixels.

2. **Unified Era-3b models keep closing on proprietary generation.** Mamoda2.5's AR-Diffusion DiT-MoE matches Kling O1 on video editing at 3B active parameters, reinforcing the May trend (LLaDA2.0-Uni) of non-pure-AR unified architectures competing directly with closed systems on generation, not just understanding.

3. **3D becomes a data/representation problem, not an architecture problem.** VLM3 shows that focal-length unification + text-based pixel reference + data scaling turns an ordinary VLM into a native 3D learner (depth 0.84→0.90) without new modules — echoing the broader "curate/represent better, don't re-architect" theme from the previous report's data-curation findings.

4. **KV-cache efficiency specializes by workload.** FastOCR exploits *temporally sparse* attention for document parsing (98% accuracy at 5% of visual tokens/step, 3.0× faster), while WindowQuant does window-level mixed-precision KV quantization for video. Efficiency work is shifting from generic token pruning toward modality- and task-aware cache strategies.

5. **Driving VLAs push both performance and efficiency frontiers.** Following last cycle's human-surpassing MindVLA-U1, Fast-dDrive (block-diffusion, 12× throughput, −22% L2) and CLOVER (94.5 PDMS NAVSIM SoTA via closed-loop ranking) show the driving stack maturing toward deployable latency and closed-loop reliability rather than open-loop scores alone.

6. **Reasoning consistency emerges as a runtime safety signal.** "Lost in Fog" finds that when a driving VLA's explanation changes under sensor perturbation, trajectory deviation jumps 5.3× — making explanation stability a cheap, label-free reliability monitor. This connects interpretability directly to operational safety.

7. **Medical VLMs hit a hallucination wall, quantified.** HalluCXR shows 61.9–82.3% of chest-radiograph outputs hallucinate, with clinically dangerous errors up to 80.2%. Combined with last cycle's MedThinkVQA multi-image ceiling, clinical deployment is now bottlenecked by *trustworthiness*, not raw capability — and ensembling/verbosity-monitoring are early mitigations.

8. **Video benchmarks keep exposing the perception gap.** TOC-Bench (temporal object consistency) and VGenST-Bench (synthesized controlled scenes) both show models that pass general video QA failing once identity-tracking or genuine spatio-temporal reasoning is isolated — continuing the "video scores ≠ video understanding" reckoning surfaced by Video-Oasis/VideoZeroBench.

9. **Small GUI agents go SFT-free.** LiteGUI distills 2B/3B GUI agents to large-model parity using on-policy distillation + dual-level GRPO with no SFT stage, extending the "smaller + more autonomous" GUI-agent trend toward cheaper training pipelines.

10. **Embodied agents gain memory and self-awareness.** POLAR personalizes embodied MLLM agents via a multimodal semantic/episodic memory graph, while Health-Conditioned VLA makes policies aware of their own hardware degradation. Embodied VLAs are starting to model *the user* and *themselves*, not just the scene.
